About Our Company

VI-Chrome was established with one goal in mind : to reduce the suffering of those who are visually impaired. VI-Chrome was founded by 4 members who shared a singular goal. Once our team was established, we started thinking of our company branding. And that’s where we come up with the name VI-Chrome. The term VI in our company’s name stands for the “visually impaired” means that our company is focused and aims to achieve universal peace and sustainability for the visually impaired. “Chrome” refers to the colour that most people visually impaired see in their daily lives. Currently, our team consists of 5 people, covering all Cheif Officers works. Our past projects was Sensurr, a glass to help the Visually Impaired cross the road and we are currently working on developing an app that can act as a personal optometrist for the user.
